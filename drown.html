Created by Jonas Margraf between Berlin, Paris, Los Angeles and Riyadh, Drown In Ur Presence is the first digital-only release on Ōtium. Based on his CGI experimentations and musical works, we teamed up to develop an enhanced digital experience that you can explore here.

Drown in Ur Presence is critical fusion. Post-digital songs, born out of curiosity and earth-shattering moments. Flickery synths, glimmers of industrial rhythm and iridescent textures are coaxed into a sensitive and incisive vision of a highly-emotive pop record.

Jonas’s creative practice is defined by similarities in process—techniques such as feedback loops, walls of noise and purely synthetic textures are applied to both audio & video contents. By discovering common characteristics across sounds, shapes and colors, he subtly gives perspective to the audiovisual correspondence of this all-encompassing piece.
